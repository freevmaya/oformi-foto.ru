How to add a cutout to the photo frame

A clear example of how to create additional cut-outs for a photo in your frame design in the application "Cool decoration of your photos", which is available in the social network "My World".

  • In the right menu, move the cursor over the item "Additional elements"
    •                 
  • In the pop-up menu, select "Add vignette (cutout below the photo)"
    •                 
  • Choose the vignette you liked in the opened dialog
    •                 
  • Using the mouse, drag it to the edited postcard
    •                 
  • Using the mouse, you can change the size and position of the cutout
    •                 
  • You can choose from an album, or upload another photo to the cutout
    •                 
  • To continue editing the position of photos in other cutouts and vignettes, click on the element you want.

Several cut-outs for photos

An example of how to embed photos in a frame with several cutouts under the photo in the application "Cool decoration of your photos", which is available in the social network «Мой мир».

  • Select a photo for the first cutout (the current cutout is framed by a flashing frame)
  • Click on the second cut-out
  • Select a photo from the album or download from your computer
  • Adjust the position and size of photos
