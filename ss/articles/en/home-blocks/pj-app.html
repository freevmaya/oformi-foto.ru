Choose a frame or template to design a photo from the template list. When the frame opens, you can upload your photo. To do this, double click on the cutout or click the "Insert your photo" button next to the cutout. Select a photo from your computer. Using the mouse, edit the position of your photo in the cutout. Save the result to the computer, click the arrow button pointing down on the rectangle symbolizing the container or floppy disk. Look video instruction
